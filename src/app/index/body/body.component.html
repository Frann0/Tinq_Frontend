TINQ, the worlds first anonymous social media app, with focus on mental health training

Tinq, strives to help you, help you by enabling you to share your thoughts on our platform anonymously with the whole world.
The concept is based around a writing technique called Expressive Writing, with which you learn to
process your thoughts on a whole other level.
Think of Tinq as an online diary where you can share your thoughts and see others thoughts about subjects completely anonymously.

Features

Community

Join our ever growing community of thinkers. This platform allows you to express your self, without the normal stress that comes with a standard social media platform.

Help your self

Tinqs main focus is allowing you to help your self. We offer courses on how to write to help your self process your thoughts in a special way. We use a technique called Expressive Writing with which you will learn about your self. Of course this is not the only way to use the platform. You can use the platform any way you'd like, within reason of course. You can share your thoughts with the world, completely anonymously. And maybe, gain some insight on some subjects you might not have thought of, or gain a new perspective on said subject. The world (and this platform) is your oyster. Go share your thoughts with the world!

Not a normal Social Media platform

Share with us, an atypical Social Media Platform, with more focus on you instead of some online persona. We are all guilty of getting a dopamine rush off of likes. But not here, this is for you, by you.
